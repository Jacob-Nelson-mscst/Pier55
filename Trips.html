MN Landscape Arboretum

Monday, May 16. 8:30am-3:30pm

Cost: $75.00 member / $85 nonmember

Every season holds its magic, and especially so at the MN Landscape Arboretum! Spring dances with vibrant color as 35,000 tulips emerge, joined by daffodils and hyacinths. Ride the tram to tour the grounds. Enjoy time to shop, explore the gardens and eat. Purchase lunch in the café, or bring your own. (Eat in one of the picnic areas, terraces or benches in the gardens.) Indulge in "Chocolate: Diversity and Quality", a guided chocolate tasting. Experience the differences among chocolates of varying qaulities, flavors and marketing angles. Learn about chocolate's journey from bean to bar and chocolate's health benefits.

Registration and payment due by April 13.
