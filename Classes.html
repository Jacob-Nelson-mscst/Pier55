Parkinson Wellness Recovery (PWR)

Tuesdays • 3-4 p.m.

Instructor: Patty Svien, Physical Therapist

Cost: $35, limited to 8 participants

PWR is an evidence-based exercise program designed

to assist people with mild to moderate Parkinson’s disease

to improve their posture, mobility, ability, endurance,

strength and function. Participants need to be

able to get up and down from the floor independently

and should bring a Yoga mat to class. To register, call

Patty at 651-380-8752.
