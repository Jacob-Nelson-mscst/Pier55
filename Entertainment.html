Silver Tones -Choral Group

The Silver Tones practice Tuesdays at 1:00 pm at Pier 55.

The fourth Tuesday of each month they meet at First Lutheran Church in Lake City. Director Coleen Fowler and accompanist Cheryl Hemphill invite you to join.

Whether you are writing your memories, wanting to explore your creative side, or just getting out and try something new, this group is for YOU! Each meeting includes activities and sharing time. The at-mosphere is fun and respectful. Join us! Calling ahead is greatly appreciated!
