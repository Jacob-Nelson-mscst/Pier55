Hold Your Next Event at Pier 55

When you rent a room from RWAS, you support community programming to keep our seniors physically, socially, emotionally and mentally active! We host support and service groups, and medical equipment lending. We also offer a huge variety of trips and programs, including: fitness classes, meditation, day trips, local hikes, card playing, walking, healthy eating, casino trips, international trips, yoga, special events and more!
