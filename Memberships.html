Membership Levels and Fees

Individual Membership - $37.00

Individuals 55 years of age or older may support RWAS, Inc. by paying $37. You will receive the monthly newsletter, a membership card, special fees for programs and trips plus discounts with local businesses. A contribution of $370 makes you a member of RWAS for life!

Household Membership - $62.00

A household (up to 2 people) may support the RWAS by paying $62. One individual in a household must be 55 years of age or older. A household may be 2 spouses, partners, or siblings. You will receive the monthly newsletter, individual membership cards, special fees for programs and trips, plus discounts with local businesses. By contributing $620, you will be members of RWAS for life.

Associate Membership - $22.00

Not yet 55? You can support RWAS by paying $22 for an Associate Membership. You will receive the monthly newsletter. If you are caregivers, you will find that the newsletter provides information regarding services and programs that may be beneficial and interesting trips you can take together.

Sustaining Membership - $25.00 or more

Add $25 or more to any membership to become a Sustaining Member. The additional amount is viewed as a donation towards operating funds for RWAS.
