Volunteers Needed

Put your time and talents to use helping others and the Senior Center. Many volunteer opportunities are available. Some of them are listed below. If you have special skills you would like to share, please give us a call and let us know: 651-327-2255.

Building Maintenance

Be part of a team helping with the overall maintenance and upkeep of Pier 55. Projects may involve inside or outside work. Handy men and women are encouraged to call Kim or Tonya at 651-327-2255.

Annadee's Closet

Annadee's thrives many happy volunteers! Annadee's has a new volunteer position available: Personal Greeter! Welcome shoppers with a smile, answer questions and be an extra set of eyes. Help is still needed in sorting, cleaning, cashiering, and more, as well. Help others, help Red Wing Area Seniors, and feel good!

Mayo Clinic Valet Drivers

Mayo Clinic provides valet parking for patients. Volunteers work Monday through Friday, two shifts each day from 8:30 to 4:30. All that is needed is a valid driver’s license and a background check. Training is provided. If interested, please call Barb Diercks at 651-267-5068.

Hospice Volunteer's

Volunteers are needed in the Red Wing area to spend time with patients who are terminally ill. If interested and/or want more information please call Lee Ann McKinney, Heartland Hospice Volunteer Coordinator: 507-993-2044 or leeann.mckinney@hcrmanorcare.com

Thank You

Thank you to our wonderful volunteers! There are so many people who volunteer their time and talents at Pier 55 and Annadee’s Closet for Red Wing Area Seniors, Inc. They answer phones, take registrations, lead groups, sort clothes, clean medical equipment, price items, do tax returns, shovel sidewalks, teach classes, work on committees, bake cakes and so much more in service to Red Wing area seniors. Our most sincere appreciation!

Meals on Wheels

Seminary Home’s Meals on Wheels program is looking for volunteers to pick up meals in Red Wing and deliver to clients in the area. Meals are delivered daily, Monday through Friday between 11:30 and 12:30 pm. This rewarding volunteer opportunity is a great way to help your neighbors. Contact Sarah Peters: 651-327-2090.
