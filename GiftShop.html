Pier 55 Gift Shop

Stop in and look at all of our handmade goods like hats, mittens, blankets, rugs, hand carved wooden figurines and much more! All of the products are made by our local seniors, some of their goods are donated but others are for your enjoyment. Half of all proceeds go to the makers themselves, while some goes towards funding Pier 55.
